## What Weekend 2 Will Build On

Weekend 2 covers generative AI foundations, platform strategy, and the three horizons framework. The instructor will use your team's preliminary strategic options as the context for applying these new frameworks.

Teams that arrive with clearly stated options get individual coaching. Teams that arrive without options do general exercises that are less valuable.

The hypothesis log and competitive landscape are also used in Weekend 2 to build the business case skeleton and ROI model. Evidence gaps identified during Sprint 1 become urgent in the context of financial modelling. Do not defer evidence gathering to the weekend.
