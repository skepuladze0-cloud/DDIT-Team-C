# Midterm Sprint Plan

## Sprint window
April 5 to May 23, 2026

## Purpose

This sprint turns Weekend 2 deliverables into a complete, polished midterm presentation. The midterm is a 25 to 30 slide executive presentation plus a supporting business case document, due before Weekend 3 begins.

## Weekly breakdown

| Week | Dates | Required work | Standard for completion |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | April 6 to 12 | Research sprint: fill three biggest open questions from business case skeleton. Each needs one named primary source. | Each question has a named finding and a cited source. |
| 2 | April 13 to 19 | Draft sections 2, 3, 4 (Industry, AI, Platform). Conclusion headlines finalised. | Sections 2 to 4 at client-presentable quality. All slides have conclusion headlines. |
| 3 | April 20 to 26 | Draft sections 5, 6 (Strategic options, business model). ROI model refined. | Strategic options section complete. Recommendation defended with evidence. |
| 4 | April 27 to May 3 | Draft sections 7, 8 (Roadmap, ROI). Full deck assembled. | Full 25-slide deck exists. Team reviews for logical flow and headline quality. |
| 5 | May 4 to 10 | Internal dry run: 25 min + 10 min hard questions. Identify three weaknesses. | Dry run completed. Three specific improvements identified and assigned. |
| 6 | May 11 to 17 | Final revisions. Complete supporting document. Prepare for Q&A. | Deck at presentation standard. Supporting document drafted. |
| 7 | May 18 to 22 | Final polish and submission prep. | Submit to Teams folder by Thursday May 22 at 18:00. |

## Team meeting cadence

Minimum two meetings per week for Weeks 1 to 4. Minimum three meetings per week for Weeks 5 to 7.
