# Strategic Problem Statement Worksheet

## Purpose

A project without a clear problem statement drifts. This worksheet forces the team to define the strategic challenge precisely.

---

## Part 1. Context

- Industry: Logistics and Transportation Industry
- Focal company or client: Georgian Post
- Primary disruption force: Rapid growth of cross-border e-commerce and increasing control of logistics by global platforms and international providers
- Why this matters now: E-commerce volumes are growing quickly, and international players are strengthening their logistics capabilities, which is putting pressure on local companies to adapt before they lose a significant share of deliveries


---

## Part 2. Current position

Describe the client's current position.
- Strengths: Strong national coverage, established infrastructure, and high brand recognition in Georgia
- Vulnerabilities: Limited technological capabilities compared to global competitors, slower operational processes, and less flexibility in adapting to new market demands
- Strategic tension: Georgian Post must modernize and compete with fast-moving, technology-driven international players while still operating within its existing infrastructure and resource constraints
---

## Part 3. Core strategic problem

Write one sentence.

### Strong format
How should [client] respond to [specific disruption force] in order to [strategic objective] while managing [main constraint]?

### Draft
- Problem statement: How should Georgian Post respond to the rapid growth of cross-border e-commerce and increasing dominance of international logistics networks in order to strengthen its position in last-mile delivery while managing operational and resource limitations?

---

## Part 4. What this problem is not

List one or two nearby questions that look related but are not the central question.

1. How can Georgian Post simply reduce delivery prices to compete with international companies?
2. How can Georgian Post improve only internal efficiency without addressing market-level changes?

This helps keep scope clean.

---

## Part 5. Success criteria

If the team answers this question well, what should become clearer?

- Criterion 1: Clear understanding of how Georgian Post can remain competitive in the evolving e-commerce logistics market
- Criterion 2: Identification of the key actions needed to increase parcel volumes and improve service performance
- Criterion 3: Defined strategic direction that balances growth opportunities with operational constraints
